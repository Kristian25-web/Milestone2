    Who am I?

    My name is Kristian Nieves! I am a third year IT student at Kennesaw State Univeristy. I'm born in Alaska but raised and in Columbia, South Carolina. I've always loved sports growing up which is my inspiration for this research project. As I've grown in my life I've seen athletes in a perspective that is greater than what the athelete produces in their sport. In my research, I have come to the belief that we can learn a great deal from athletes with how they approach their sport. How can you approach life in the same way a point gaurd has to react to a defesive scheme? How can you make the most out of your opportunities as if the coach gave you a play to set in motion?
