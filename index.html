    Welcome!

    This project will walk the user through the lifestyle of an athlete. The athlete's covered in this project will be NFL Quaterback Jalen hurts, WNBA Center A'ja Wilson, and NBA Point Gaurd Shai Gilgeous-Alexander. By looking at these athletes who are at the top of their respective sports, the user will learn about their training, their dominance in their sport, and how they've succeeded. With that, the purpose of this project is to be open to anyone, not just sports fans. The user will also learn how this lifestyle can create applicable and successful habits that can translate in their field. With topics focused on mindset, goals, visualization, etc.

    • Training Regimen
    • Translation to the sport
